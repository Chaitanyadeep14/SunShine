Precision in Every Cut,

Excellence in Every Part

CNC (Computer Numerical Control) and VMC (Vertical Machining Center) machining are advanced manufacturing techniques used for high-precision cutting, drilling, and shaping of materials.

  • High Precision - Ensures accuracy with tight tolerances.
  • Automation - Reduces human error and improves efficiency.
  • Versatility - Suitable for automotive and industrial applications.
  • Cost-Effective - Optimizes material usage and reduces waste.

Strong Molds,

Stronger Results.

Die casting is a high-precision metal manufacturing process where molten metal is injected into a mold under high pressure to create strong, durable, and complex components with excellent surface finish.

  • High Accuracy - Produces intricate shapes with tight tolerances.
  • Strength & Durability - Ensures high structural integrity.
  • Cost-Effective - Ideal for mass production with minimal waste.
  • Smooth Finish - Reduces the need for additional machining.

Turning Ideas into Reality

with Precision Lathes

At Sunshine Engineering Works, we utilize advanced lathe machines to manufacture custom precision components that meet industry standards. Lathe machining is a versatile metal shaping process where a workpiece rotates against a cutting tool to create cylindrical parts.

  • High Accuracy – Ideal for creating symmetrical parts.
  • Versatile Applications – Used in automotive and manufacturing.
  • Smooth Finishing – Ensures refined surface quality.
  • Durable & Reliable – Suitable for small and large-scale production.

Drill with Accuracy,

Tap with Strength

Drilling and tapping are essential machining processes used to create precise holes and internal threads in metal and other materials. It removes material to form a hole, while tapping cuts threads inside the hole for screws and bolts.

  • Precision Hole Creation – Ensures accurate positioning and depth.
  • Strong Threading – Enhances assembly strength and durability.
  • Versatile Applications – Used in industrial manufacturing.
  • High Efficiency – Reduces machining time for mass production.

Shaping Strength with

Hydraulic Results.

A hydraulic press is a high-power machine that uses hydraulic pressure to apply force for bending, forming, molding, and pressing metal components. It is widely used in automotive, aerospace, and heavy machinery industries for shaping and assembling parts.

  • High Force Output – Handles heavy-duty pressing with ease.
  • Precision & Control – Ensures uniform pressure for accurate shaping.
  • Versatile Applications – Used for metal forming and molding.
  • Durability & Efficiency – Reduces waste and improves productivity.
